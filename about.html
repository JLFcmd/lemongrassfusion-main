About Lemongrass Fusion

Located on the beautiful coast of Mijas, Lemongrass Fusion is more than just a restaurant; it's a culinary journey. We specialize in bringing the authentic flavors of Asia and blending them with the freshness of the Mediterranean.

Our team of chefs works tirelessly to create dishes that not only nourish the body but also captivate the senses. From our vibrant Thai Curries to our innovative signature creations, every dish is designed to surprise and delight you.

We invite you to relax on our terrace, enjoy a handcrafted cocktail, and let yourself be carried away by the Lemongrass experience.
